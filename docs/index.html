Phage Dwelling Temperatures - Black Rock City 2019

Comparison of dwelling temperatures at The Phage, 4:00 & Esp, Burning Man 2019

An examination of thermodynamics in a variety of playa dwellings:

  • baseline outdoor recording (Taken from Altitude Lounge @ 845&C)
  • shiftpod 1 (A/C, no shade)
  • shiftpod 2 - mjp (light use of swamp cooler, shade structure)
  • shiftpod 2 - pnelson (heavy use of swamp cooler, shade structure, blastshield)
  • H12 hexayurt (no cooling)

Temperatures over the week

Temperatures fluctuated a fair amount over the course of the week but were fairly mild overall. The measured outside high temperature was 96.1°F, and the low was 54.1°F.

Average 24 hour temperature by dwelling

As Marty has known for ages, it is better to just sleep outside. On average, the hexayurt stays warmer at night. If we had a recording of a yurt with a swamp cooler or an A/C, this would easily be the most comfortable dwelling.

The Shiftpod2 in the shade with "aggressive" use of a swamp cooler stayed much cooler during the heat of the day than the other Shiftpod2 (also in the shade). The Shiftpod1 in the sun was using an A/C, which also strongly decreased the slowly rising morning temperature.
